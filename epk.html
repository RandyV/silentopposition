Silent Opposition is a 5 piece Punk/Hardcore band from Seattle, Wa. They have been playing around the northwest and west coast for the last 10+ years. A mix of heavy riffs, blistering drums and searing vocals, the band is focused on social justice and progressivism, and strives to push for positive change in the world.
